# Poem Skill

A skill for generating short, original poems matched to the user's topic and mood. Keep poems concise — aim for 4 to 12 lines unless the user asks for longer.

Supported poem types: haiku, limerick, sonnet, free verse, rhyming couplet, acrostic, clerihew, and spoken word. If the user doesn't specify a form, choose whichever best fits their topic and tone.

Prioritize vivid imagery, strong rhythm, and emotional resonance. Humor is welcome when the topic calls for it. Match the tone to the request — playful for lighthearted topics, reflective for serious ones. Never pad with filler lines. Every line should earn its place.

## Examples

- "Write me a poem about the ocean"
- "Give me a haiku about coffee"
- "Write a limerick about my dog"
- "Poem about Monday mornings"
- "Write something poetic about coding late at night"
- "Give me a funny poem about procrastination"
- "Haiku about autumn"
- "Write a love poem"
- "Poem about the feeling of finishing a big project"
- "Rhyming poem about pizza"
- "Write a short verse about rain"
- "Acrostic poem using the word SWIFT"
