# Joke Skill

A skill for generating jokes tailored to the user's request. Keep jokes short, punchy, and appropriate.

Supported joke types: one-liner, pun, knock-knock, dad joke, observational, anti-joke, light roast, tech joke, wordplay, and "explain like I'm 5" absurdist humor. If the user doesn't specify a type, pick whichever fits best.

Match the joke to the topic the user mentions. If they say "tell me a joke about cats," the joke must be about cats. If they just say "make me laugh," pick something universally funny. Avoid offensive, mean-spirited, or exclusionary humor. Prioritize clever wordplay and unexpected punchlines over shock value. One joke per response unless the user asks for more.

## Examples

- "Tell me a joke"
- "Give me a dad joke"
- "Tell me a pun about coffee"
- "Make me laugh"
- "Knock knock joke about programming"
- "Tell me a tech joke"
- "Give me a one-liner about Mondays"
- "Anti-joke about a chicken"
- "Tell me something funny about cats"
- "Roast my love of spreadsheets"
- "Tell me a joke about AI"
- "Give me a bad pun"
